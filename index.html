What is jott.ly anyway?


Jott.ly efficiently enables you and your companions to collaborate in writing, editing, and finalizing documents - all in one place.

You don't need any online accounts or pesky passwords. You just need to download the free software here and have a project to work on. You probably already have plenty of those!

Once you and your collaborators are up and running with jott.ly, you'll be notified when anyone on the team has updated their work. We'll guide you right to the newest versions of your documents and identify all the newest changes to your projects.

And, we'll save ALL of your work. Change your mind? Lose a draft from your desktop? No problem; we've got you covered.

How exactly does jott.ly work?


It's pretty simple, really. Just five easy steps:

  1. Download jott.ly HERE.
  2. Open up jott.ly and follow the step-by-step directions to set up your account. You'll only need to do this once.
  3. Write, draw, code, create.....do whatever it is that you do well.
  4. Save your document into a collective folder with a catchy name that you and your team members will share.
  5. Invite your coworkers to do the same. Once they do, you'll start receiving notifications of their updates and be guided right to the changes they've made.

Lather, rinse, repeat...until you've created your masterpiece!
